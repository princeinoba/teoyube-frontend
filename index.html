    Theology & Safety Guardrails

    Built into this prototype

    • Every promise card shows Scripture references and keeps Teoyube words as memory aids, not replacements for Scripture.
    • Calling language stays provisional: patterns may point toward a direction, but the app does not declare final destiny.
    • Major decisions should include prayer, wise counsel, fruit, Scripture, and user agency.
    • Promises are never marked fulfilled automatically. Testimony belongs to the Saint.
